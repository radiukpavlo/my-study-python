\begin{ex} Let $M=\left\{ \vect{u}=\left( u_{1},u_{2},u_{3},u_{4}\right) \in 
\mathbb{R}^{4}:\left| u_{1}\right| \leq 4\right\} .$ Is $M$ a subspace of $\mathbb{R}^4$?
\begin{sol}
This is not a subspace. $\leftB \begin{array}{r}
1 \\
1 \\
1 \\
1
\end{array}
\rightB $ is in
it, but $20\leftB 
\begin{array}{r}
1 \\
1 \\
1 \\
1
\end{array}
\rightB $ is not. 
\end{sol}
\end{ex}

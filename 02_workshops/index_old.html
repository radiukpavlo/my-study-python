Table of Contents

  • Preface
    Summary: There were 44 papers submitted for peer-review to this workshop from authors representing 8 countries. Each submission underwent a rigorous double-blind peer-review process by at least two independent PC members. Out of these, 15 papers were accepted for this volume, resulting in an acceptance rate of approximately 34%.

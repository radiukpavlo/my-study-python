\begin{ex} Suppose $\left\{ \vect{x}_{1},\cdots ,\vect{x}_{k}\right\} $ is a
set of vectors from $\mathbb{R}^{n}.$ Show that $\vect{0}$ is in $\func{
span}\left\{ \vect{x}_{1},\cdots ,\vect{x}_{k}\right\} .$
\begin{sol}
$\sum_{i=1}^{k}0\vect{x}_{k}=\vect{0}$
\end{sol}
\end{ex}
